# User Request
> the top section containing Level info, Moves, Pushes etc and Kile/Dislike... Make these more compact, ie.. reduce their total height.

# Implementation Details

To make the top header section more compact, several padding, margin, and text size adjustments were made in `activity_game.xml`:

1. **Header Padding**: Reduced the main `LinearLayout` padding from `12dp` to `4dp`.
2. **Text Sizes**: 
   - Reduced level title from `20sp` to `18sp`.
   - Reduced prev/next arrows from `24sp` to `20sp` and their padding from `8dp` to `4dp`.
   - Reduced Moves/Pushes/Time texts from `16sp` to `14sp`.
   - Reduced Like/Dislike emojis from `24sp` to `20sp` and padding from `8dp` to `4dp`.
3. **Margins**: 
   - Reduced divider top/bottom margins from `8dp` to `4dp`.
   - Reduced the Like/Dislike row top margin from `12dp` to `4dp`.

These changes significantly compress the vertical height of the top card while remaining readable and tappable.
