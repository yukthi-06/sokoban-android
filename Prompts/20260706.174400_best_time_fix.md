# Changes Made
- Prevented the `timerRunnable` from overwriting the best time when a solved game is initially loaded (`movesCount == 0`).
- Changed `startTime = System.currentTimeMillis();` to execute on the *first move* instead of on level load, ensuring the timer stays perfectly still at 0s (or the best time) until the player actually begins playing.
